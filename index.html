Projects

Heart Disease Analysis with POWERBI

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of heart disease trends across various demographics. It explores patterns among 9,500 patients, highlighting key health indicators and risk factors such as age, gender, smoking habits, and diabetes.

Key Insights Extracted:
  • 20.03% of total patients were diagnosed with heart disease.
  • Disease prevalence increases significantly in older age groups.
  • Males and females show a nearly equal heart disease ratio.
  • Smoking and diabetes have clear associations with higher heart disease risk.

Interactive filters (Gender and Age Group) enable dynamic exploration of data, helping medical teams or analysts quickly identify high-risk populations and improve prevention strategies.

E-commerce Sales Analysis using SQL

This SQL project explores a large Pakistan E-commerce Dataset to uncover valuable business insights using advanced SQL queries and aggregation techniques. The goal was to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, and customer behavior.

Key Insights Extracted:
  • Calculated total sales by month and year to identify high-performing periods.
  • Determined top 3 months with the highest total sales.
  • Measured overall revenue using aggregate functions.
  • Built queries to find average order value (AOV) and unique customers per month.

The project demonstrates strong command over SQL concepts such as GROUP BY, ORDER BY, aggregate functions, and data-driven insight extraction — crucial skills for data analysis roles.

Auto Sales Analysis with Power BI

This Power BI project analyzes a comprehensive dataset from the global automobile industry to extract actionable business insights. Using advanced Power BI visualizations and DAX calculations.

Key Insights Extracted:
  • Aggregated total revenue, sales volume, and average price to visualize overall business scale over time.
  • Calculated average engine size and average mileage for deeper product insights.
  • Created a combined column and line chart to show annual total sales alongside year-over-year (YoY) percentage change for the last 15 years.
  • Identified trends and anomalies—such as sales spikes, declines, and recoveries, useful for strategy refinement.

The dashboard showcases proficiency in data modeling, DAX measures, and visual storytelling with Power BI.
